## 7. Public Records (public-records.html)

### Public Records Request
- **Email address for public records requests:** records@springfield.gov

### Public Record Fees
- **Retrieval Fee (per page or per hour):** $0.25 per page
- **Copy Fee (per page):** $0.10 per page
- **Other Fees:** $15 per hour for staff time over 1 hour

### FAQ Responses
- **How much do public records cost?** First hour of staff time is free, then $15/hour. Copies are $0.10/page
- **How long to retrieve records?** Most requests fulfilled within 5 business days
- **Can I inspect records in person?** Yes, by appointment
- **What if my request is denied?** You will receive written explanation and may appeal to City Manager

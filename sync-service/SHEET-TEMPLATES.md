## Quick Start Checklist

1. ✅ Create 7 Google Sheets with names above
2. ✅ Copy column headers (first row) exactly as shown
3. ✅ Fill in your municipality's data
4. ✅ Share all sheets with service account email
5. ✅ Get sheet IDs from URLs
6. ✅ Update `simple_sync_config.json` with sheet IDs
7. ✅ Run sync: `python simple_sync.py --config simple_sync_config.json --once`
8. ✅ Check generated files in `/components/` and `/assets/js/`

## Tips

- **Start with Municipality Config** - This is the most important!
- **Column names must match exactly** - Case and spelling matter
- **Sheet tab names must match exactly** - Use the names shown above
- **Empty rows are skipped** - It's okay to have gaps
- **Update anytime** - Just edit the sheet and re-run sync
- **Test with small data first** - Add 1-2 rows to test before filling everything in

Doing Business Here: We support local businesses and welcome new enterprises to our community!

Starting a Business

Follow these steps to start your business in our city:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Business Plan: Develop your business concept and plan
  2. Business Structure: Choose legal structure (LLC, Corporation, etc.)
  3. Business Name: Register your business name with the state
  4. Federal EIN: Obtain Employer Identification Number from IRS
  5. City Business License: Apply for city business license
  6. Zoning Approval: Verify location is properly zoned
  7. Building Permits: Obtain required construction/renovation permits
  8. Health Permits: Get health department approval (if applicable)
  9. Fire Inspection: Schedule fire safety inspection
  10. Open for Business: Launch your business!

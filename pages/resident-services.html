Voter Registration & Elections

Register to Vote

Register to vote or update your voter registration:

  • Online: Register at state elections website
  • In Person: City Clerk's Office, City Hall
  • By Mail: Download and mail registration form
  • Deadline: 30 days before election

Voting Information

  • Find Polling Place: Enter address on county elections website
  • Early Voting: 2 weeks before election day
  • Absentee Ballots: Request online or by mail
  • Voter ID: Valid photo ID required

Upcoming Elections

  • May 7, 2025: Municipal Election
  • November 4, 2025: Special Election

Vehicle Services

Vehicle Registration

Register your vehicle and renew tags:

  • New Residents: 30 days to register vehicle
  • Renewal: Annual renewal before expiration
  • Required Documents: Title, insurance, ID, registration fee
  • Location: County Tag Office, 500 Government Plaza

Driver's License

Update your driver's license address:

  • New Residents: 60 days to obtain state license
  • Address Change: Update within 30 days of move
  • Location: DMV Office, 600 License Drive

Parking Permits

  • Residential Parking: Required in designated zones
  • Visitor Permits: Temporary permits available
  • Accessible Parking: Disabled parking placards

Vital Records

Birth Certificates

Order certified copies of birth certificates:

  • Fee: $15 per copy
  • Processing Time: 5-7 business days
  • Required: Valid ID and relationship verification
  • Methods: Online, in-person, or by mail

Death Certificates

Order certified copies of death certificates:

  • Fee: $15 per copy
  • Processing Time: 5-7 business days
  • Required: Valid ID and relationship verification

Marriage Licenses

Apply for a marriage license:

  • Fee: $50
  • Valid: 30 days from issue date
  • Required: Both applicants present with valid ID
  • Waiting Period: None
  • Office Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM
Order Vital Records

Library Services

The City Library offers books, media, programs, and resources for all ages!

Library Card

Get a free library card:

  • Available to all residents
  • Valid ID and proof of address required
  • Instant card issue

Services Available

  • Book checkout (3-week loan)
  • eBooks and audiobooks
  • DVD and music rentals
  • Computer and internet access
  • Meeting rooms
  • Children's programs
  • Adult education classes

Library Locations

  • Main Library: 300 Library Lane

  • South Branch: 2000 South Avenue

Hours: Monday-Saturday 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM

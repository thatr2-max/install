Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

The City is committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information obtained through this website.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Complete online forms
  • Make online payments
  • Subscribe to newsletters
  • Create an account
  • Submit service requests
  • Send us email

This may include: name, address, email, phone number, payment information, and other details you provide.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited
  • Time and date of visit
  • Referring website

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide requested services
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Respond to inquiries
  • Send notifications and updates
  • Improve website functionality
  • Analyze website usage
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and security threats

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information:

  • With Other City Departments: As necessary to provide services
  • With Service Providers: Third parties who assist with website operations (payment processors, hosting services)
  • As Required by Law: In response to subpoenas, court orders, or legal processes
  • Public Records: Some information may be subject to public records laws

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Maintain login sessions
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Improve user experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some website features may not function properly.

Third-Party Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. Google Analytics uses cookies and collects data anonymously. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Employee training on data protection

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to your information
  • Opt-out of email communications
  • Request deletion of your account (subject to retention requirements)

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@citygovernment.gov

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Public Records

Information submitted to the City may be subject to public records laws. Certain information may be disclosed in response to public records requests, subject to applicable exemptions.

External Links

Our website contains links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Please review their privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

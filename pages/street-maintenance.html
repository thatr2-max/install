Snow Removal

The city's snow removal operations keep roads safe and passable during winter weather.

Snow Removal Priority Routes

Priority 1

Major arterials, highways, emergency routes

Cleared first during snow events

Priority 2

Collector streets, bus routes, hills

Cleared after Priority 1 routes

Priority 3

Residential streets

Cleared after Priority 1 and 2

View Snow Route Map

Snow Removal Operations

Snow plowing begins when accumulation reaches 2 inches. Crews work 24/7 during snow events.

What We Do

  • Plow and salt main roads first
  • Clear residential streets after main roads
  • Remove snow from downtown business district
  • Clear snow from fire hydrants and catch basins

What Residents Can Do

  • Park cars off streets during snow events
  • Do not push snow into streets
  • Clear sidewalks adjacent to your property
  • Keep fire hydrants clear of snow
  • Be patient - crews work as quickly as safely possible

Sidewalk Maintenance

Property owners are responsible for maintaining sidewalks adjacent to their property.

Property Owner Responsibilities

  • Keep sidewalks in good repair
  • Remove snow and ice within 24 hours of snowfall
  • Trim vegetation encroaching on sidewalks
  • Repair damaged sidewalks

City Hall Location

Address: 123 Main Street, Anytown, USA 12345

Accessibility: City Hall is fully wheelchair accessible with:
  • Accessible parking spaces
  • Elevator to all floors
  • Accessible restrooms
  • Assistive listening devices
  • Service animal friendly

Parking Information

  • Free parking in City Hall lot (200 spaces)
  • 10 accessible parking spaces near entrance
  • Street parking available (2-hour limit)
  • Public parking garage 1 block away

Public Transportation

  • Bus Routes 5, 12, and 18 stop at City Hall
  • Bus stop is wheelchair accessible
  • Metro station 2 blocks away

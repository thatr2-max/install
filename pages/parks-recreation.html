Recreation Programs

Year-round programs for all ages and abilities!

Youth Programs

  • Youth Sports Leagues: Soccer, baseball, basketball, volleyball (ages 5-17)
  • Summer Camps: Day camps, sports camps, arts camps (ages 6-14)
  • After School Programs: Homework help, enrichment activities
  • Swimming Lessons: All skill levels (ages 3+)
  • Youth Dance: Ballet, jazz, hip-hop

Adult Programs

  • Fitness Classes: Yoga, Pilates, Zumba, aerobics
  • Adult Sports Leagues: Softball, basketball, volleyball
  • Swimming: Lap swim, water aerobics
  • Art Classes: Painting, pottery, photography
  • Wellness Programs: Health screenings, nutrition classes

Senior Programs (55+)

  • Senior Center: Daily activities and social programs
  • Fitness Classes: Chair yoga, tai chi, water aerobics
  • Day Trips: Monthly excursions
  • Lunch Program: Hot meals Monday-Friday
  • Cards & Games: Bridge, bingo, mahjong

Accessibility

We are committed to providing accessible recreation opportunities for all!

  • Facilities: All parks and recreation facilities are ADA accessible
  • Programs: Adaptive programs available for people with disabilities
  • Equipment: Accessible playground equipment at all major parks
  • Trails: Paved, accessible trails throughout the system
  • Parking: Accessible parking at all locations
  • Assistance: Staff available to assist with accommodations

Contact us at (555) 123-4560 to discuss specific accessibility needs.

Permit Types & Requirements

Building Permits

Required for construction, renovation, or structural changes.

  • New construction
  • Additions & renovations
  • Electrical work
  • Plumbing work
  • HVAC installation

Processing Time: 5-10 business days

Fee: Varies by project scope

Business Licenses

Required to operate a business within city limits.

  • New business registration
  • License renewal
  • Home-based business
  • Food service license
  • Professional services

Processing Time: 3-5 business days

Fee: $50-$500 annually

Special Event Permits

Required for public events and gatherings.

  • Street festivals
  • Parades
  • Block parties
  • Outdoor concerts
  • Farmers markets

Processing Time: 30 days minimum

Fee: $100-$1,000

Pet Licenses

Required for all dogs and cats within city limits.

  • Dog licenses
  • Cat licenses
  • Rabies vaccination required
  • Annual renewal
  • Senior citizen discounts

Processing Time: Same day

Fee: $10-$25 annually

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a permit?

Processing times vary by permit type. Simple permits may be approved same-day, while complex building permits can take 5-10 business days.

Can I track my application status online?

Yes! Use the application status checker above with your application number to track progress.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, checks, money orders, and cash (in person only).

Do I need an inspection?

Most building, electrical, and plumbing permits require inspections. Our office will contact you to schedule inspections after permit approval.

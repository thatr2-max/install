Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Contact us at (555) 123-4567 or submit a question.

General City Services

What are City Hall hours?

City Hall is open Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on federal holidays.

How do I contact a specific department?

Visit our Staff Directory for department contact information and phone numbers.

Where do I pay my city bills?

Pay bills online at our payment portal, by phone at (555) 123-4520, by mail, or in person at City Hall.

How do I report a problem in my neighborhood?

Use our Report an Issue system online or call (555) 123-4530 during business hours.

How can I get involved in city government?

Attend City Council meetings, join a city board or commission, or volunteer for city programs.

Billing & Payments

When is my water bill due?

Water bills are due on the 15th of each month. Late fees apply after the due date.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards (2.5% fee), eCheck ($0.50 fee), auto-pay (free), checks by mail (free), and cash/check in person (free).

Can I set up a payment plan?

Yes! Contact our Finance Department at (555) 123-4520 to discuss payment plan options for property taxes or large balances.

Why is my water bill high?

High bills often indicate a leak. Check your toilets, faucets, and irrigation system. Call (555) 123-4530 for a meter check.

How do I set up auto-pay?

Visit our payment portal and click "Enroll in Auto-Pay" or call (555) 123-4520.

Permits & Licenses

Do I need a permit to build a fence?

Fences over 6 feet tall require a building permit. All fences must meet zoning setback requirements.

How long does it take to get a building permit?

Simple permits: 1-3 days. Complex building permits: 5-10 business days depending on plan review requirements.

Do I need a business license?

Yes, most businesses operating within city limits require a business license. Apply at our Permits page.

How much does a business license cost?

Business license fees range from $50-$500 annually depending on business type and size.

Do I need to license my pet?

Yes, all dogs and cats must be licensed. Fees are $10-$25 annually. Apply at Permits.

Trash & Recycling

When is my trash pickup day?

Collection days vary by zone. Check our Public Services page or call (555) 123-4530 to find your collection day.

What if I missed my trash pickup?

If you missed collection, containers will be picked up the following week. Ensure containers are at the curb by 6:00 AM on your collection day.

What can I recycle?

We accept paper, cardboard, plastics #1-7, metals, and glass. See our recycling guide for details.

How do I dispose of large items?

Bulk item pickup is available the first Saturday of each month. Call (555) 123-4530 to schedule.

Where can I drop off hazardous waste?

The Hazardous Waste Drop-off Center is open Saturdays 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM at the Public Works facility.

Streets & Infrastructure

How do I report a pothole?

Report potholes through our online system or call (555) 123-4530. Most are repaired within 2-5 business days.

How do I report a streetlight outage?

Report outages online or call (555) 123-4530. Include the pole number if visible. Repairs typically take 1-3 business days.

When does the city plow snow?

Priority routes (main streets, emergency routes) are plowed first, followed by residential streets. Plowing begins when snow reaches 2 inches.

Can I request a speed bump on my street?

Submit a traffic calming request to Public Works. Requests are evaluated based on traffic studies and safety criteria.

Who maintains the sidewalks?

Property owners are responsible for sidewalk maintenance and snow removal on sidewalks adjacent to their property.

Parks & Recreation

How do I reserve a park shelter?

Reserve shelters online or call (555) 123-4560. Reservations can be made up to 6 months in advance.

Do I need a permit for a special event in a park?

Yes, special events require a permit. Apply at least 30 days in advance through our permit system.

Are dogs allowed in city parks?

Yes, leashed dogs are welcome in all parks. We also have a fenced dog park at Riverside Park.

How do I register for recreation programs?

Register online, in person at the Community Center, or call (555) 123-4560. View programs at Parks & Recreation.

What are Community Center hours?

Monday-Friday 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM, Saturday 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM, Sunday 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM.

Public Safety

How do I get a police report?

Request police reports in person at the Police Department or online. Some reports are available immediately; others take 3-5 business days.

Can I file a police report online?

Yes, for non-emergency incidents like minor theft, vandalism, or lost property. Visit our Public Safety page.

Do you offer CPR classes?

Yes! The Fire Department offers monthly CPR and first aid classes. Cost is $30 per person. Call (555) 123-4550 to register.

How do I schedule a fire inspection?

Contact the Fire Department at (555) 123-4551 to schedule required inspections for businesses and multi-family buildings.

How do I sign up for emergency alerts?

Register at our Public Safety page to receive emergency notifications via text, email, and phone.

Planning & Development

How do I find my property's zoning?

Use the address lookup tool on our Planning & Zoning page, view the online zoning map, or call (555) 123-4570.

Can I operate a business from my home?

Home occupations are permitted in residential zones with restrictions. Contact Planning & Zoning at (555) 123-4570 for requirements.

How do I request a zoning change?

Submit a rezoning application. The process takes 60-90 days and includes public hearings. See Planning & Zoning for details.

Do I need a permit for a sign?

Most commercial signs require a permit. Contact Planning & Zoning at (555) 123-4570 or see our permit information.

Voting & Elections

How do I register to vote?

Register online, in person at the City Clerk's office, or by mail. Registration deadline is 30 days before the election.

Where is my polling place?

Find your polling place by entering your address on the county elections website or calling (555) 123-4567.

Can I vote early?

Yes! Early voting begins 2 weeks before election day at designated early voting locations.

How do I get an absentee ballot?

Request an absentee ballot online through the county elections office or by calling (555) 123-4567.

Website & Technology

Is the city website accessible?

Yes! Our website is designed to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. View our Accessibility Statement for details.

How do I report a website problem?

Report technical issues or accessibility barriers through our accessibility feedback form or email it@citygovernment.gov.

Can I pay bills on my mobile phone?

Yes! Our website is mobile-friendly and all payment options work on smartphones and tablets.

How do I create an online account?

Click "Create Account" on any login page and follow the registration process. You'll need your account number and email address.

Employment

How do I apply for a city job?

All applications must be submitted online through our Careers page. View current openings and apply electronically.

Does the city offer internships?

Yes! We offer paid summer internships and semester internships. Visit Careers for information.

What benefits does the city offer?

We offer health insurance, dental, vision, retirement pension, paid time off, holidays, and professional development. See employee benefits.

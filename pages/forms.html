How to Submit Forms

Online Submission

Many forms can be submitted online for faster processing. Look for "Apply Online" or "Submit Online" buttons.

By Mail

Mail completed forms to:
City Hall
123 Main Street
Anytown, USA 12345

In Person

Bring completed forms to City Hall during business hours:
Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

By Email

Some forms can be emailed to the appropriate department. Check the form instructions for email addresses.

Form Assistance

Need help completing a form?

  • In Person: Visit City Hall for assistance
  • By Phone: Call (555) 123-4567
  • Email: info@citygovernment.gov
  • Accessibility: Alternative formats available upon request

Document Notarization

Notary services are available at City Hall:

  • Cost: $5 per signature
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Location: City Clerk's Office, 1st Floor
  • Required: Valid photo ID

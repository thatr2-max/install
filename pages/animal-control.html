All dogs and cats over 4 months old must be licensed.

Requirements

  • Current rabies vaccination certificate
  • Proof of spay/neuter (for reduced fee)
  • Owner information

Animal Ordinances

Leash Law

All dogs must be on a leash when off owner's property. Maximum leash length: 6 feet.

Pet Limits

  • Residential properties: Maximum 4 dogs and/or cats
  • Additional pets require special permit

Noise Violations

Excessive barking or animal noise may result in citations.

Dangerous Animals

Animals deemed dangerous must be properly contained and display warning signs.

Report Animal Issues

Spay/Neuter Programs

Low-cost spay/neuter services available for qualifying residents:

  • Income-Based Assistance: Reduced cost surgery for low-income households
  • Voucher Program: Apply for surgery vouchers
  • Community Clinics: Monthly low-cost clinics

Call (555) 123-7387 for more information.

Wildlife Management

For wildlife issues (raccoons, opossums, squirrels, etc.):

  • Animal Control does not trap or relocate wildlife
  • Contact a licensed wildlife removal service
  • Never approach or feed wild animals
  • Secure trash cans to prevent wildlife access

Development Applications

Various applications are required for development and land use changes:

Rezoning

Request to change property zoning classification.

Review Time: 60-90 days

Fee: $500-$1,500

Process: Staff review, public hearing, Planning Commission, City Council

Conditional Use Permit

Permission for special uses in zoning district.

Review Time: 45-60 days

Fee: $300-$750

Process: Application, staff review, public hearing

Variance

Exception to zoning requirements (setbacks, height, etc.).

Review Time: 30-45 days

Fee: $250-$500

Process: Application, Board of Appeals hearing

Site Plan Review

Required for new commercial/industrial development.

Review Time: 30-60 days

Fee: Based on project size

Process: Submit plans, staff/commission review

Subdivision

Divide land into multiple lots for development.

Review Time: 60-120 days

Fee: $1,000-$5,000

Process: Preliminary and final plat approval

Sign Permit

Required for most commercial signs.

Review Time: 5-10 days

Fee: $50-$300

Process: Submit application and design

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find my property's zoning?

Use the address lookup tool above, view our online zoning map, or call our office at (555) 123-4570.

Do I need a permit for a fence?

Fences over 6 feet tall require a building permit. Check zoning regulations for setback requirements.

How long does the rezoning process take?

Typically 60-90 days from application to City Council decision, including public hearings.

Can I operate a business from my home?

Home occupations are permitted in residential zones with certain restrictions. Contact our office for details.

When is a site plan required?

Site plans are required for most new commercial, industrial, and multi-family developments.

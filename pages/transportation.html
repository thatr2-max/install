Accessibility Features

  • All buses wheelchair accessible
  • Priority seating for seniors and disabled
  • Audio and visual stop announcements
  • Bike racks on all buses
  • ADA paratransit service available
  • Service animals welcome

Paratransit Service

Door-to-door service for eligible individuals who cannot use regular bus service.

  • Eligibility: ADA certification required
  • Reservations: Call 24 hours in advance
  • Cost: $3.00 per trip
  • Phone: (555) 123-RIDE (7433)

Real-Time Information

  • Mobile App: Download CityTransit App
  • Text Alerts: Text stop number to 41411
  • Online: Real-Time Tracker
  • Phone: (555) 123-INFO

Bike & Ride

  • All buses equipped with bike racks (2 bikes)
  • Bike parking at transit centers
  • Bike share program integration

Park & Ride

Free parking lots for transit users:

  • North Park & Ride: 200 spaces - Routes 2, 4
  • South Park & Ride: 150 spaces - Routes 2, 3
  • East Park & Ride: 100 spaces - Route 3

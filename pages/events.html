Accessibility Services

We are committed to ensuring all community members can participate in city events:

  • Physical Accessibility: All event venues are wheelchair accessible
  • Parking: Accessible parking spaces available at all events
  • Sign Language: ASL interpreters provided upon request (48-hour notice)
  • Assistive Listening: Devices available at public meetings
  • Materials: Large print and braille materials available
  • Service Animals: Welcome at all city events
  • Dietary: Alternative food options available when meals are served

To request accommodations, contact us at (555) 123-4560 or events@citygovernment.gov at least 48 hours before the event.

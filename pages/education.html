School District Overview

The City School District serves over 15,000 students in grades Pre-K through 12.

District Facts

  • Schools: 12 Elementary, 3 Middle, 2 High Schools
  • Students: 15,234
  • Teachers: 982
  • Student-Teacher Ratio: 15:1
  • Graduation Rate: 94%

Mission Statement

To provide every student with a high-quality education that prepares them for success in college, career, and life while fostering critical thinking, creativity, and character development.

School Enrollment

Enroll your child in our public schools for the upcoming school year.

Enrollment Requirements

To enroll, you will need:

  • Proof of residency (utility bill, lease agreement)
  • Birth certificate or passport
  • Immunization records
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Photo ID of parent/guardian

Registration Process

  1. Complete online pre-registration form
  2. Gather required documents
  3. Visit your zoned school to complete registration
  4. Attend orientation (dates provided at registration)

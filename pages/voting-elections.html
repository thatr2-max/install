Voter Registration

Register to vote or update your voter registration information.

Eligibility Requirements

To register to vote, you must:

  • Be a U.S. citizen
  • Be at least 18 years old by election day
  • Be a resident of the county
  • Not be a convicted felon (unless rights restored)
  • Not be declared mentally incapacitated by a court

Absentee Voting

Vote by mail if you cannot vote in person on election day.

Who Can Vote Absentee

  • Military members and their families
  • Citizens living overseas
  • College students away from home
  • Voters who will be out of county on election day
  • Persons with disabilities
  • Voters age 65 or older

How to Request Absentee Ballot

  1. Submit absentee ballot application
  2. Receive ballot by mail
  3. Complete and return ballot by deadline

Application Deadline: October 29, 2025

Ballot Return Deadline: November 5, 2025 at 7:00 PM

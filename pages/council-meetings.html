How to Speak at a Meeting

  1. Sign Up: Complete a speaker card before the meeting or sign up online
  2. Time Limit: Each speaker has 3 minutes to address the Council
  3. Topic: Comments may be on any topic or on specific agenda items
  4. Respect: Please be respectful and avoid personal attacks

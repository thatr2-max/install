Payment Assistance Programs

Financial assistance may be available for eligible residents:

  • Senior Citizen Property Tax Exemption: Residents 65+ may qualify for reduced property taxes
  • Low-Income Utility Assistance: Help with water and sewer bills for qualifying households
  • Payment Plans: Installment plans available for property taxes and large balances
  • Hardship Programs: Special programs for residents facing financial difficulties

Contact our Finance Department to learn more about assistance programs.

Frequently Asked Questions

When are property taxes due?

Property taxes are due in two installments: April 1 and October 1. Late payments incur a 1.5% monthly penalty.

Can I view my payment history?

Yes! Create an online account to view payment history, download receipts, and manage your accounts.

What if I can't pay my bill in full?

Contact us at (555) 123-4520 to discuss payment plan options.

How do I dispute a charge?

Submit a dispute form online or call the appropriate department listed on your bill.

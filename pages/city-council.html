About the City Council

The City Council is the legislative body of the city government, responsible for making policy decisions, passing ordinances, and approving the city budget.

Council Structure

  • 7 Council Members: Elected from districts to 4-year terms
  • Mayor: Elected at-large to 4-year term
  • Regular Meetings: First and third Tuesday of each month
  • Work Sessions: Second Tuesday of each month

Council Responsibilities

  • Adopt city budget and tax rates
  • Pass ordinances and resolutions
  • Approve contracts and agreements
  • Appoint board and commission members
  • Set policy direction for city
  • Represent constituents' interests

Public Comment

Citizens have several opportunities to address the City Council.

Public Comment Period

Each meeting includes a public comment period where any citizen may address the Council on any topic.

  • Time Limit: 3 minutes per speaker
  • Sign-Up: In-person sign-up sheet before meeting starts
  • Virtual Option: Submit written comments online

Public Hearings

Required for specific items such as:

  • Budget adoption
  • Zoning changes
  • Special use permits
  • Annexations

Public hearings allow extended time for public input on specific issues.

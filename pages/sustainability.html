Recycling & Composting

What to Recycle

  • Paper: Newspapers, cardboard, office paper, magazines
  • Plastics: Bottles and containers #1-7
  • Metals: Aluminum and steel cans
  • Glass: Bottles and jars (all colors)

Composting Program

Reduce waste by composting food scraps and yard waste.

  • Free compost bins for residents
  • Yard waste collection
  • Community composting sites
  • Composting workshops

Residential Programs

Solar Panel Rebates

Incentives for installing solar panels on your home:

  • Up to $2,000 rebate
  • Property tax exemption
  • Net metering available

Energy Audits

Free home energy assessments to identify savings opportunities.

Schedule Energy Audit

Rain Barrel Program

Purchase discounted rain barrels for water conservation.

  • $30 per barrel (50-gallon)
  • Installation instructions provided
  • Reduces stormwater runoff

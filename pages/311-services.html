About 311

311 is your single point of contact for non-emergency city services. Our customer service representatives are available 24/7 to help with:

  • Service requests
  • City information
  • Department transfers
  • Complaint reporting
  • General questions

What Happens After You Call

  1. Request Logged: Your request is entered into our system with a unique tracking number
  2. Routed: Request is sent to the appropriate department
  3. Assigned: Department assigns request to field staff
  4. Resolved: Issue is addressed according to service standards
  5. Closed: Request is marked complete in the system

You can track your request status online using your tracking number.

When NOT to Call 311

For emergencies, always call 911:
  • Police emergency
  • Fire
  • Medical emergency
  • Crimes in progress
  • Life-threatening situations

Frequently Asked Questions

Is 311 available 24/7?

Yes! You can call 311 any time, day or night. Some requests may not be processed until the next business day.

Can I track my request?

Yes! Use your tracking number to check status online or call 311.

Do I need to provide my name?

No, anonymous requests are accepted. Providing contact information allows us to update you on status.

What if my request isn't resolved?

Call 311 with your tracking number and we'll investigate the status.

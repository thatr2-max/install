Application Process

  1. Search Openings: Browse current job postings above
  2. Review Requirements: Ensure you meet the minimum qualifications
  3. Submit Application: Complete the online application form
  4. Screening: Applications are reviewed by HR and hiring managers
  5. Testing: Some positions require written tests or skills assessments
  6. Interview: Qualified candidates are invited for interviews
  7. Background Check: Final candidates undergo background screening
  8. Job Offer: Successful candidates receive a conditional offer
  9. Onboarding: New employees complete orientation and training

Veterans Preference

The City provides preference points to eligible veterans in the hiring process:

  • 5 points for honorably discharged veterans
  • 10 points for disabled veterans
  • Verification of military service required
  • Submit DD-214 with your application

Accessibility & Accommodations

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a job?

All applications must be submitted online through our job portal. Click "View Details & Apply" on any job posting.

Can I apply for multiple positions?

Yes! You may submit separate applications for each position you're interested in.

How long does the hiring process take?

The process typically takes 4-8 weeks from application deadline to job offer, depending on the position.

Do you accept walk-in applications?

No, all applications must be submitted online. If you need assistance, visit our HR office during business hours.

What is the residency requirement?

Some positions require city residency within 6-12 months of hire. Check individual job postings for details.

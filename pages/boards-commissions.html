Application Process

  1. Check Requirements: Must be city resident, 18+ (unless Youth Council)
  2. Complete Application: Fill out board/commission application
  3. Submit Application: Online, email, or in person
  4. Council Review: City Council reviews all applications
  5. Interview: Council may interview candidates
  6. Appointment: Council votes on appointments
  7. Orientation: New members attend orientation

Responsibilities

  • Attend all scheduled meetings
  • Review meeting materials in advance
  • Participate in discussions
  • Represent diverse community perspectives
  • Act in best interest of the city
  • Follow ethical guidelines and avoid conflicts of interest

  • Public comment periods provided

Frequently Asked Questions

Are board members paid?

No, board and commission positions are volunteer. Some boards may reimburse travel expenses.

How much time commitment is required?

Most boards meet monthly for 1-2 hours. Additional time may be needed for review of materials.

Can I serve on multiple boards?

Generally no, to allow more citizens to participate. Exceptions may be granted.

What if I can't attend a meeting?

Notify the staff liaison. Excessive absences may result in removal from the board.

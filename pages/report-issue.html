What Happens After You Report?

  1. Submission: Your report is received and assigned a tracking number
  2. Review: City staff reviews your report and assigns it to the appropriate department
  3. Assessment: Field staff assesses the issue and determines the required action
  4. Resolution: Work is scheduled and completed
  5. Notification: If you provided contact information, you'll receive updates via email

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to provide my name?

No, anonymous reports are accepted. However, providing contact information allows us to update you on the status of your report.

How long does it take to fix issues?

Response times vary by issue type and severity. Emergency issues are addressed immediately, while routine maintenance may take several days.

Can I attach photos?

Yes! Photos help our staff better understand and address the issue. You can upload photos when submitting your report.

What if the issue isn't resolved?

Contact us at (555) 123-4530 with your request number, and we'll investigate the status.

Public Services

Essential City Services

The City provides essential utility and sanitation services to all residents and businesses.

Water & Sewer Services

Water Service

  • Water Quality: City water meets all federal and state standards
  • Billing: Monthly billing based on meter readings
  • New Service: Call (555) 123-4530 to start service
  • Emergencies: Report water main breaks 24/7 at (555) 123-9999

Sewer Service

  • Billing: Included in monthly water bill
  • Maintenance: City maintains sewer lines in street; property owner maintains service line
  • Backups: Report sewer backups immediately

Conservation Tips

  • Fix leaky faucets and toilets
  • Install low-flow fixtures
  • Water lawn early morning or evening
  • Use dishwasher and washing machine with full loads

Storm Water Management

The City maintains storm drains and infrastructure to prevent flooding.

Help Prevent Flooding

  • Keep storm drains clear of leaves and debris
  • Never dump anything down storm drains
  • Report clogged drains to (555) 123-4530
  • Maintain your property drainage

Flood Safety

  • Never drive through flooded areas
  • Move to higher ground during flooding
  • Sign up for emergency alerts
  • Have an emergency plan

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have a water emergency?

Call our 24-hour emergency line at (555) 123-9999 for water main breaks, sewer backups, or other urgent issues.

Why is my water bill high?

High bills may indicate a leak. Check toilets, faucets, and irrigation systems. Call (555) 123-4530 for a meter check.

What happens if I don't put my trash out on time?

Containers must be out by 6:00 AM. Late containers will be collected the following week.

Can I get an extra recycling bin?

Yes! Additional recycling bins are available at no charge. Call (555) 123-4530 to request one.
